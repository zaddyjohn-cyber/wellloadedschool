Privacy Policy

How Welloaded Schools collects, uses, and protects your information.

📅 Last Updated: June 30, 2026
ℹ️
Summary

We collect minimal data, we do not sell your information, and we only use cookies for analytics and advertising. By using this website, you agree to this policy.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Welloaded Schools ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at wellloadedschools.com, including any other media form, media channel, mobile website, or mobile application related or connected to it.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with the terms of this policy, please stop using our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Subscribe to our email newsletter
  • Submit a contact form
  • Submit an opportunity for us to review
  • Leave a comment on our content (if enabled)

This information may include your name, email address, country of residence, and the content of your message.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information is collected automatically, including:

  • Your device type, operating system, and browser type
  • Your IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Clicks and navigation behaviour on our website

This information is collected using cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 5 below).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you our newsletter with scholarship alerts, exam updates, and education guides (only if you subscribed)
  • To respond to your contact form messages and enquiries
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To understand which pages and articles are most helpful to our readers
  • To display relevant advertisements through ad networks (see Section 6)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will never use your information for purposes not listed above without your explicit consent.

4. Email Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address will be stored securely with our email marketing provider. We will only send you education-related content including scholarship alerts, exam updates, grant notices, and education guides.

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send. We will remove your email address promptly upon request.

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience and help us understand how visitors use the site.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g. dark mode preference, session state).
  • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics to track page views, traffic sources, and user behaviour in an anonymised way.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by advertising networks (such as Google AdSense) to display relevant advertisements to visitors.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

6. Advertising

We may display advertisements on our website through third-party advertising networks, including Google AdSense. These networks use cookies to show you ads that may be relevant to your interests based on your browsing activity across websites.

We do not control the content of these advertisements or the data collected by advertising partners. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using a browser privacy extension.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external websites, including official scholarship portals, government websites, university websites, and other third-party resources. Once you leave our website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for students and young people aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at hello@wellloadedschools.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate information.
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to unsubscribe: Opt out of our email newsletter at any time.
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@wellloadedschools.com.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@wellloadedschools.com
  • Website: wellloadedschools.com/contact

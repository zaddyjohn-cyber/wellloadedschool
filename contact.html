Common Questions

Do you charge students to apply for scholarships? +
No. Welloaded Schools is completely free. We do not collect scholarship applications and we never charge students any fees. We only provide information and direct you to the official scholarship website.
How do I report wrong information? +
Use the contact form below and select "Report Wrong Information" as your reason. Please include the article title and what you believe is incorrect. We take accuracy seriously and will review and update the content promptly.
Can I submit a scholarship or grant for you to cover? +
Yes! We welcome opportunity submissions. Use the contact form below and select "Submit an Opportunity." Please include the official link so we can verify it before publishing.
